About Us

What's
the BluePass?

The UAE BluePass provides the direct conection to the relevant players of the UAE marine industry, including global shipping lines, ship owners, the ports and waters of the UAE.

Why is BluePass consideted
a transformational project for the UAE?

The BluePass initiative will increase the competitiveness of the UAE's Maritime sector by promoting value-added services and products to participating stakeholders and transiting ships.

Introduction

The BluePass aims to bring together maritime companies, ship management companies, ship owners, shipyards, yachts, and recreational and other entities related to the UAE Maritime sector.

The mission

By joining the BluePass initiative, your company will access various benefits that will undoubtedly enhance your operations in the UAE.
